Brigada Eskwela of San Francisco High School



BRIGADA:

Immerse yourself in the vibrant spirit
of unity and community engagement at
San Francisco High School in Quezon
City as we embark on our Brigada
Eskwela journey in Bago Bantay. Witness
the dedication of our students, teachers,
and parents as we come together
to prepare our school for the upcoming
academic year. From cleaning classrooms
to beautifying the surroundings, every hand
plays a crucial role in creating a conducive
learning environment for all. Join us in
upholding the Filipino tradition of bayanihan and
experience the transformative power of teamwork
and collaboration. Watch our Brigada
Eskwela video now and be inspired
by our shared commitment to excellence
in education.
