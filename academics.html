ACADEMICS IN SAN FRANCISCO HIGH SCHOOL



San Francisco High School in
Quezon City, situated in Barangay Sto Cristo, Misamis St.
is a reputable public institution known
for its commitment to academic excellence and
holistic student development. With a focus
on providing quality education, the school
offers a range of academic programs tailored
to meet the diverse needs and interests
of its student body. Beyond academics,
San Francisco High School fosters
a supportive learning environment, encouraging students
to participate in extracurricular activities and community engagement
initiatives, thus preparing them for future
success in their chosen endeavors.
